From the Pilot

StoryCare helps patients externalize internal experiences.

These stories were created during group sessions as part of the initial pilot.

Why It Works

Externalization builds agency

Turning experience into narrative and image helps patients see problems as separate from identity.

Reflection deepens integration

Revisiting structured narrative between sessions strengthens insight and memory consolidation.

Symbolic media expands processing

Visual and auditory media engage emotional systems beyond verbal dialogue.

Visible engagement informs care

Patterns of return, response, and reflection provide real signal for the next session.

Clinical Framework

A flexible structure the clinician leads.

Eight modules. Individual or group. Measurement built in.

Patients move through a structured sequence: from safety and orientation, through narrative development, to meaning-making and integration. At each step, they create something, reflect on it, and carry it forward. The default protocol is 8 modules. It adapts to individual or group formats.

Designed across populations and settings. Each session is recorded, transcribed, and turned into a Story Page with built-in outcome measures.

  • 01 Orientation & Safety
  • 02 Identity Anchoring
  • 03 Narrative Development I
  • 04 Narrative Development II
  • 05 Meaning Making
  • 06 Future Projection
  • 07 Integration
  • 08 Closure & Continuity

StoryCare is HIPAA-compliant with end-to-end encryption, role-based access, and audit logging. Audio, transcripts, and generated media are stored securely and never used to train AI models.
StoryCare is currently in clinical evaluation and available to clinicians and researchers interested in early adoption. It's designed for residential, outpatient, and community settings working with group or individual therapy.
The patient securely logs into their Story Page, a structured narrative built from session transcripts. It includes therapist-curated videos, music, images, reflection prompts, and surveys, all grounded in what the patient shared.
StoryCare was built around Digital Narrative Therapy, but the core tools transcription, media generation, Story Pages, and outcome tracking can support other structured therapeutic approaches.
