StoryCare gives therapists the tools to turn a therapy session into a personal Story Page: film, music, images, reflection prompts, and surveys. Each one is built from what the patient shared. The patient receives it on their phone and engages with it between sessions.

Short Film AI-generated images animated into video scenes with transitions and subtitles
Original Music Composed to match the emotional arc of each story. New music every time.
Reflection Clinician-selected prompts that connect session themes to daily life
Measurement Surveys, engagement tracking, and emotional signals per patient per module

The Process
How therapy becomes tangible.
01

Share

Patients talk. A therapist listens and guides. The session is recorded with consent.

02

Shape

The therapist works in StoryCare to select key moments and shape each patient's narrative.

03

Create

The platform generates images, video, and original music from what was shared. The therapist refines and publishes each Story Page.

04

Respond

The patient receives their Story Page. They watch, listen, reflect, and respond between sessions.

Clinical Framework
Grounded in evidence. Structured for scale.

StoryCare delivers Digital Narrative Exposure Therapy (DNET), an 8-module protocol that draws on Narrative Exposure Therapy, Narrative-Enactment and Creative Therapy, and Yalom's group therapeutic factors. These are established approaches to trauma processing, identity work, stigma reduction, and group cohesion.

Narrative therapy has always been hard to standardize and harder to measure. The digital layer changes that. The platform records, transcribes, generates media, and captures outcomes at every step, without flattening the clinical work. The protocol is transdiagnostic: PTSD, substance use disorders, depression, and schizophrenia-spectrum conditions.

  • 01 Self-Resilience and Re-Authoring
  • 02 Grounding and Regulation
  • 03 Relational Healing and Integration
  • 04 Forgiveness and Letting Go
  • 05 Becoming
  • 06 Acceptance and Continuity
  • 07 Direction
  • 08 Sustaining Change
Research & Access
Currently in trials. Open to clinicians.

StoryCare is an emerging digital therapeutic, currently in clinical trials. We're studying how structured narrative therapy combined with AI-generated media affects trauma symptoms, quality of life, and patient engagement.

It's built for real clinical settings residential, outpatient, community. Training is short. The Therapist Guide covers session flow, prompts, and reflection for every module.
